Taco Pasta

Description: Tasty alternative to tortilla tacos!

Ingredients

  • Chicken mince 400-600g
  • Pasta
  • Creamcheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Taco Spice
  • Cheese
  1. Cut the chicken into small pieces
  2. Have some oil into a pan and set it to high heat
  3. When the pan is hot add the chicken
  4. While chicken is warming up, prepare rice on the side
  5. Cut the paprika and add it in the pan along with the chicken
  6. When the chicken is properly cooked, add 4-5 teaspoons of tandoori spice
  7. Mix the spice evenly
  8. Add 4 table spoons of Crémé Fréche and let it sit on medium heat for 3-4 minutes
  9. Dinner is ready to be served!
