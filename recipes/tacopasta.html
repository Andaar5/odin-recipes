Taco Pasta

Description: Tasty alternative to tortilla tacos!

Ingredients

  • Chicken mince 400-600g
  • Pasta
  • Creamcheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Taco Spice
  • Cheese
  1. Start boiling water for the pasta
  2. Heat the chicken mince in a pan
  3. Boil pasta until it's ready
  4. When the mince is done cooking, add creamcheese
  5. When creamcheese is mixed, add sour cream
  6. When sour cream is mixed and pasta is done add it all into a ildfast form
  7. Add cheese on top
  8. Have it in the oven until the cheese is evenly melted and golden
  9. Dinner is ready to be served!
