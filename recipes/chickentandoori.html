Odin Recipes: Chicken Tandoori

Home Chicken Tandoori Taco Pasta

Description: This is a healthy and easy-to-make dish, packed with protein, ready in just 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 400g chicken
  • 1 serving of rice
  • 4 tablespoons of Crème Fraîche
  • Paprika
  • Tandoori spice
  • Oil for Cooking
  1. Cut the chicken into small pieces
  2. Heat some oil in a pan over high heat.
  3. Once the pan is hot, add the chicken and cook until browned.
  4. While the chicken is cooking, prepare the rice on the side.
  5. Cut the paprika into small pieces and add it to the pan with the chicken.
  6. Once the chicken is fully cooked, add 4-5 teaspoons of tandoori spice and mix well to coat the chicken evenly.
  7. Add 4 tablespoons of Crème Fraîche to the pan and stir. Let it cook on medium heat for 3-4 minutes.
  8. Your dish is ready to serve!
